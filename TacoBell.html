TACO BELL

In addition to Show Choir, another thing that I really, really enjoy is Taco Bell. It is definitely my favorite fast food place, and while it's probably not the best for you, I eat there WAY too much. What are my favorite things on the menu? Chalupas, Baja Blast Freezes, Cinnamon Twists, Doritos Locos Tacos, the list goes on.
